Moonsun Ale Company is a brewery based in London

Moonsun Ale Company opened its doors in the fall of 2014. With a shared love of beer and the sprit of community that can be found in having a brew in the company of others, co-owners rachel and caroline wanted to create a beer that always was refreshing and full of flavor. Since 2014 Moonsun has developed and perfected

five beers that they beleive embody what they first set out to create. With seasonal limited edition creations, and the tried and true crafted brews, Moonsun has come to be a favorite for many. Moonsun is continually looking to explore new flavors so that they can bring you new and refreshing ale
